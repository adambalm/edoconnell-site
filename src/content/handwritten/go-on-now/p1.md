# Handwriting, Quran, and Tawdry Creation

Help. God almighty. Jesus,
would you look at that?
My handwriting has worsened.
I'd blame the pen, but its
not even really a pen. Its
more of a magnet. And
this screen is no more
a piece of paper than I
am an aztec. The
Quran submits itself
as testimony and evidence
of its author's provenance.
To what will my tawdry
creation testify?
